# Iteration trong python
    - Iteration la mot khai niem chung cho viec lay tung phan tu cua mot doi tuong nao do, 
    bat cu khi nao ban su dung mot vong lap hay ky thuat nao do de co duoc gia tri mot nhom 
    phan tu thi do chinh la Iteration
    - Iterable object la mot object co phuong thuc __iter__ tra ve mot Iterator,
    hoac la mot object co phuong thuc __getitem__ cho phep ban lay bat cu phan tu 
    nao cua no bang indexing vi du nhu CHUOI, LIST, TUPLE
    - Iterator object don gian chi la mot doi tuong ma cho phep ta lay tung gia tri mot cua no.
    Co nghia la ban khong the lay bat ky gia tri nao nhu lam voi Chuoi hay LIST
    - Cac Iterable object chua phai la Iterator. Khi su dung ham iter se tra ve mot Iterator.
    Day cung chinh la cach ma cac vong lap hoat dong.
